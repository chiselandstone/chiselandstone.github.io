About Ronnie

Email Me 214.493.8008

Welcome to Chisel & Stone. My name is Ronnie Johnson and I'm a husband, designer, & coffee enthusiast living and working in sunny Dallas, TX. Chisel & Stone was born out of a desire to capture and explore life through a camera lens.


My hope for this endeavor is always produce honest and inspiring photos that challenge perspective and thought. This is an ongoing journey and I hope you enjoy the things I create. After all, great photos are not taken—they are created.
